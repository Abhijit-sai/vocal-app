# Telangana Public Grievance Topics

Compiled from public-facing conversations and reporting across Reddit, indexed X/Twitter-style complaints, Telugu news coverage, civic grievance portals, and public accountability discussions.

Note: This is a synthesis of public grievance themes, not a statistically representative survey. X/Twitter access is limited through public indexing unless direct platform/API access is available.

## 1. Civic Infrastructure and GHMC Services

### 1.1 Drainage Overflow and Sewage on Roads

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain that drainage water overflows onto roads and into colonies, creating bad smell, mosquito breeding, health risks, and daily inconvenience.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Drainage water has been overflowing for weeks and no one is responding."
- Tinglish: "Drainage water road meeda vastundi, complaint chesthe close chestunnaru but issue same undi."
- Telugu: "డ్రైనేజీ నీరు రోడ్లపైకి రావడంతో ప్రజలు తీవ్ర ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

GHMC and HMWSSB should fix the root drainage-capacity issue, not just do temporary cleaning after complaints become visible.

### 1.2 Bad Roads, Potholes, and Dug-Up Roads

**Common complaint**

Roads are repeatedly dug up for utility work and left unrepaired. Potholes, uneven surfaces, and dust make travel unsafe, especially for two-wheelers.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Roads have become dangerous. Every few months they dig and leave it like that."
- Tinglish: "Tax kattedi enduku? Road potholes valla daily ride dangerous aipoyindi."
- Telugu: "రోడ్లపై గుంతలు, ధూళి వల్ల ప్రయాణికులు తీవ్ర అసౌకర్యానికి గురవుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Publish ward-level road repair timelines, contractor names, penalties, and actual completion status.

### 1.3 Waterlogging and Monsoon Flooding

**Common complaint**

Even short rains cause waterlogging, blocked roads, submerged colonies, and traffic collapse due to blocked nalas and weak stormwater planning.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "One rain and the whole road becomes a lake."
- Tinglish: "Rain padithe road swimming pool la aipothundi. Nala cleaning ekkada?"
- Telugu: "వర్షం పడగానే రోడ్లపై నీరు నిల్వై ప్రజలు ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Pre-monsoon nala desilting, transparent flood-risk maps, and public tracking of stormwater works.

### 1.4 Garbage Collection and Illegal Dumping

**Common complaint**

Garbage piles, irregular collection, informal payments, open dumping, and waste burning are frequent local grievances.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Garbage is not being cleared and the smell is unbearable."
- Tinglish: "Complaint pettina waste remove cheyyaru. Colony lo dumping spot aipoyindi."
- Telugu: "చెత్త డంపింగ్ వల్ల మా ఆరోగ్యం, పర్యావరణం దెబ్బతింటున్నాయి."

**Accountability ask**

Ward-wise sanitation audits, clear collection schedules, action against open dumping, and complaint closure only after citizen confirmation.

### 1.5 Streetlights and Dark Roads

**Common complaint**

Non-working streetlights create safety concerns, especially for women, students, workers returning late, and residents in inner lanes.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Streetlights have not worked for days. It is unsafe at night."
- Tinglish: "Street lights levu, night time lo bayam ga undi."
- Telugu: "వీధి దీపాలు వెలగకపోవడంతో రాత్రి సమయంలో భద్రతపై భయం నెలకొంది."

**Accountability ask**

GIS-tagged streetlights, 48-hour repair timelines, and public escalation if repeated complaints are ignored.

## 2. Water and Electricity

### 2.1 Drinking Water Scarcity and Low Pressure

**Common complaint**

Residents report low pressure, irregular supply, dependence on tankers, and unclear response from water-board helplines.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "There is no proper water supply and we are forced to depend on tankers."
- Tinglish: "Water pressure chala low undi, tanker book chesthe kuda late ga vastundi."
- Telugu: "తాగునీటి సరఫరా లేక ప్రజలు తీవ్ర ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Reliable piped supply, transparent tanker allocation, and area-wise water shortage dashboards.

### 2.2 Tanker Fraud and Private Water Charges

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain about fake or incomplete tanker deliveries, private tanker overcharging, and lack of reliable monitoring.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "The tanker did not deliver fully but the complaint was closed."
- Tinglish: "Tanker half water icharu, full bill charge chesaru."
- Telugu: "ట్యాంకర్ల ద్వారా నీటి సరఫరాలో అవకతవకలు జరుగుతున్నాయని ప్రజలు ఆరోపిస్తున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

GPS and meter-based tanker tracking, receipt verification, and penalties for fraudulent deliveries.

### 2.3 Power Cuts and Voltage Fluctuations

**Common complaint**

Power cuts during summer, voltage fluctuations, and lack of estimated restoration time are common complaints, especially from working professionals and families.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Power cuts every night in summer and no proper update from the department."
- Tinglish: "Summer lo current cut chesthe ela? WFH, kids, elders andariki problem."
- Telugu: "కరెంట్ కోతలతో ప్రజలు తీవ్ర ఇబ్బందులు ఎదుర్కొంటున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Feeder-level outage information, restoration ETAs, compensation for repeated outages, and infrastructure upgrades in high-growth areas.

## 3. Traffic, Transport, and Mobility

### 3.1 Traffic Violations and Weak Enforcement

**Common complaint**

Wrong-side driving, signal jumping, high beams, illegal parking, and inconsistent enforcement generate anger.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Rules are only for common people. Wrong-side driving is everywhere."
- Tinglish: "Wrong side driving daily jarugutundi, police action ekkada?"
- Telugu: "ట్రాఫిక్ నిబంధనలు ఉల్లంఘించిన వారిపై కఠిన చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని ప్రజలు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Consistent camera and field enforcement, penalties for official vehicles too, and public reporting follow-up.

### 3.2 Traffic Congestion and Poor Road Design

**Common complaint**

Residents blame poor junction design, closed U-turns, VIP diversions, and construction bottlenecks for worsening traffic.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Every new flyover just moves the traffic jam to another point."
- Tinglish: "U-turn close chesaru, now 2 km extra traffic lo stuck avvali."
- Telugu: "ట్రాఫిక్ రద్దీతో ప్రయాణికులు గంటల తరబడి ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Data-led traffic planning, public consultation before diversions, and independent review of large road projects.

### 3.3 Public Transport Gaps

**Common complaint**

People complain about insufficient buses, crowded services, poor bus stops, unreliable tracking, and weak metro last-mile access.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Public transport is not reliable enough for daily commute."
- Tinglish: "Bus ekkadam kuda kashtam, metro nundi last mile problem."
- Telugu: "ప్రజా రవాణా సౌకర్యాలు సరిపోక ప్రయాణికులు ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

More buses, better routes, live tracking, safer stops, and affordable last-mile connectivity.

### 3.4 Auto and Cab Overcharging

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain about autos refusing rides, overcharging, surge pricing, and lack of action on repeat offenders.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Autos refuse short rides or ask double fare."
- Tinglish: "Meter use cheyyaru, fixed high rate cheptharu."
- Telugu: "ఆటో చార్జీల పేరుతో ప్రయాణికులపై అధిక వసూళ్లు జరుగుతున్నాయని ఫిర్యాదులు వస్తున్నాయి."

**Accountability ask**

Fare enforcement, complaint-linked penalties, and better integration with public transport.

## 4. Land, Housing, and Urban Governance

### 4.1 Land Records, Dharani/Bhu Bharati, and Revenue Issues

**Common complaint**

People report land-record errors, private land being marked incorrectly, survey delays, mutation issues, and anxiety around land ownership.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "My land record is wrong and no office is giving a clear answer."
- Tinglish: "Dharani lo mistake undi, office chuttu tirugutunnam but solution ledu."
- Telugu: "ధరణి పోర్టల్‌లో ఉన్న భూమి సమస్యలను పరిష్కరించాలని ప్రజలు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Time-bound surveys, transparent correction process, and protection against land grabbing.

### 4.2 Land Grabbing and Encroachment

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain about powerful local actors occupying land, threatening owners, or manipulating records.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Land grabbers are threatening us and officials are not helping."
- Tinglish: "Maa land occupy chesaru, complaint pettina action ledu."
- Telugu: "భూకబ్జాలపై ప్రభుత్వం కఠిన చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని బాధితులు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Fast-track land-grabbing complaints, police-revenue coordination, and public case status.

### 4.3 HYDRAA Demolitions and Due Process

**Common complaint**

Public opinion is split. Some support lake and nala protection, while affected residents ask why permissions, loans, and registrations were allowed earlier.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "If it was illegal, why did officials approve it in the first place?"
- Tinglish: "Permission ichindi government, ippudu citizen house demolish chestara?"
- Telugu: "అక్రమ నిర్మాణాలపై చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలి, కానీ బాధితులకు న్యాయం కూడా చేయాలి."

**Accountability ask**

Publish FTL and buffer maps, punish approving officials, and provide notice, appeal, and rehabilitation where needed.

### 4.4 Musi River Project and Evictions

**Common complaint**

Activists and residents ask for DPR transparency, rehabilitation clarity, and protection against forced displacement.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Development should not mean displacement without rehabilitation."
- Tinglish: "Musi project kosam poor people ni evict chesthara? Rehab clarity kavali."
- Telugu: "మూసీ ప్రాజెక్ట్‌లో నిర్వాసితులకు సరైన పునరావాసం కల్పించాలని డిమాండ్ చేస్తున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Publish DPR, conduct social impact assessment, and guarantee rehabilitation before eviction.

### 4.5 Illegal Constructions and Town Planning

**Common complaint**

People complain about illegal buildings, blocked roads, construction debris, noise, and officials ignoring violations.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Illegal construction is blocking the road and no one is acting."
- Tinglish: "Builder rules follow cheyyadu, officials silence lo unnaru."
- Telugu: "అక్రమ నిర్మాణాలపై అధికారులు చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని ప్రజలు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Audit approvals, disclose violations, penalize builders and officials responsible for illegal permissions.

## 5. Welfare, Entitlements, and Public Services

### 5.1 Housing Scheme Access

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain about delays, unclear beneficiary selection, bribe demands, and no clear appeal process for rejected applications.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "We applied for housing but there is no clear status."
- Tinglish: "Illu kosam application pettam, office chuttu tirugutunnam."
- Telugu: "ఇళ్ల పథకంలో అర్హుల ఎంపిక పారదర్శకంగా ఉండాలని ప్రజలు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Public beneficiary lists, appeal mechanism, anti-bribe action, and application tracking.

### 5.2 Ration Cards and Food Security

**Common complaint**

People seek new ration cards, corrections, card activation, and action against ration-shop service lapses.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Ration card application status is unclear."
- Tinglish: "Ration card kosam apply chesam, update ledu."
- Telugu: "రేషన్ సరుకుల సరఫరాలో సేవాలోపం ఉంటే ప్రజలు ఫిర్యాదు చేయవచ్చు."

**Accountability ask**

Clear timelines, public status tracking, grievance appeal, and action against ration dealers for service lapses.

### 5.3 Pensions and Social Security

**Common complaint**

Senior citizens, widows, disabled persons, and low-income families complain of delays, rejected applications, and lack of explanation.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "My pension application is pending for months."
- Tinglish: "Pension application pending undi, reason evaru cheppatledu."
- Telugu: "పెన్షన్ దరఖాస్తులు పెండింగ్‌లో ఉండటంతో లబ్ధిదారులు ఇబ్బందులు పడుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Time-bound processing, rejection reasons, and doorstep support for vulnerable applicants.

### 5.4 Prajavani Complaint Closures

**Common complaint**

Citizens allege that complaints are marked as closed even when the real issue remains unresolved.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "The grievance was closed without solving anything."
- Tinglish: "Complaint close chesaru, issue matram same undi."
- Telugu: "ప్రజలు ఫిర్యాదు చేసినా సమస్యలు పరిష్కారం కాకుండానే మూసివేస్తున్నారని ఆరోపిస్తున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Citizen-confirmed closure, appeal for wrongly closed grievances, and department-wise closure-quality audits.

## 6. Police, Safety, and Legal Accountability

### 6.1 FIR Refusal and Poor Police Response

**Common complaint**

People report being discouraged from filing complaints, being asked to compromise, or receiving action only after social media escalation.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Police are not registering the complaint."
- Tinglish: "Station ki vellina FIR teesukovatledu, compromise cheyyamani chepthunnaru."
- Telugu: "బాధితుల ఫిర్యాదులు వెంటనే నమోదు చేసి చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని ప్రజలు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Zero FIR enforcement, complaint receipt guarantees, officer accountability, and escalation tracking.

### 6.2 Women’s Safety and Harassment

**Common complaint**

Women and families raise concerns about stalking, harassment, unsafe public places, dark streets, and weak follow-up.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Public spaces do not feel safe at night."
- Tinglish: "Night time lo street lights levu, harassment fear undi."
- Telugu: "మహిళల భద్రత కోసం పోలీసులు, స్థానిక సంస్థలు మరింత చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలి."

**Accountability ask**

Visible patrols, functioning streetlights, fast harassment response, and survivor-friendly reporting.

### 6.3 Cybercrime and Digital Harassment

**Common complaint**

Common issues include financial fraud, loan app harassment, fake utility links, morphed images, blackmail, and delayed response.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "I reported cyber fraud but the bank freeze and police process are too slow."
- Tinglish: "Scam ayindi, 1930 call chesam but money freeze status ledu."
- Telugu: "సైబర్ మోసాలపై వేగంగా చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని బాధితులు కోరుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Faster 1930 response, bank coordination, case tracking, and Telugu-language cyber awareness.

## 7. Public Health, Environment, and Quality of Life

### 7.1 Stray Dogs and Dog Bites

**Common complaint**

Residents complain about dog bites, children and elderly people being unsafe, and slow sterilization or vaccination response.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Children are scared to walk because of stray dogs."
- Tinglish: "Stray dogs chala unnayi, kids outside ki velladaniki bayapadutunnaru."
- Telugu: "వీధి కుక్కల బెడదతో ప్రజలు భయాందోళనలకు గురవుతున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Humane but effective sterilization, vaccination, bite-response teams, and public reporting by locality.

### 7.2 Pollution, Dust, and Construction Nuisance

**Common complaint**

Citizens complain about construction dust, debris, open burning, noise, and air pollution near roads and work sites.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Construction dust is affecting our health."
- Tinglish: "Dust pollution valla breathing problem avutundi, rules follow cheyyaru."
- Telugu: "ధూళి కాలుష్యంతో ప్రజల ఆరోగ్యం దెబ్బతింటోంది."

**Accountability ask**

Construction-site enforcement, debris removal, dust-control rules, and penalties for repeat violations.

### 7.3 Lake, Nala, and River Pollution

**Common complaint**

People complain about sewage entering lakes, encroached nalas, polluted water bodies, and poor enforcement.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "The lake has become a sewage dump."
- Tinglish: "Lake lo sewage vadilestunnaru, authorities action teesukovali."
- Telugu: "చెరువుల్లో మురుగు నీరు కలవడంతో పర్యావరణం దెబ్బతింటోంది."

**Accountability ask**

Stop sewage inflows, publish lake-restoration status, and enforce anti-encroachment laws consistently.

## 8. Education, Jobs, and Youth Concerns

### 8.1 Government Job Exams and TGPSC Delays

**Common complaint**

Aspirants complain about exam delays, court cases, uncertainty, cancelled exams, and lack of a predictable calendar.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Aspirants are losing years because of delays and unclear notifications."
- Tinglish: "TGPSC notification, exam, result anni delay. Youth life stuck aipoyindi."
- Telugu: "ఉద్యోగ పరీక్షల్లో ఆలస్యం వల్ల అభ్యర్థులు తీవ్ర ఆందోళన వ్యక్తం చేస్తున్నారు."

**Accountability ask**

Annual recruitment calendar, litigation-proof process, transparent evaluation, and regular updates.

### 8.2 Unemployment and Skill-Job Gap

**Common complaint**

Young people complain about lack of local jobs, low salaries, fake consultancies, and poor training-to-employment pathways.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "There are degrees but no decent jobs."
- Tinglish: "Jobs levu, consultancies fake promises chestunnayi."
- Telugu: "నిరుద్యోగ సమస్యపై ప్రభుత్వం తక్షణ చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలని యువత కోరుతోంది."

**Accountability ask**

Transparent job drives, action against fake consultancies, and outcome-based skill programs.

## 9. Cross-Cutting Accountability Themes

### 9.1 Complaint Closure Without Resolution

**Pattern**

Across civic, welfare, land, and police issues, the same frustration appears: complaint numbers are generated, but the real issue is not solved.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "They closed the complaint without fixing the issue."
- Tinglish: "Ticket close chesaru, problem same undi."
- Telugu: "సమస్య పరిష్కారం కాకుండానే ఫిర్యాదును మూసివేశారు."

### 9.2 Need for Social Media Visibility

**Pattern**

Citizens often believe authorities respond only when complaints go viral or when officials are tagged publicly.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Unless it goes viral, no one cares."
- Tinglish: "X lo tag chesthe matrame response vastundi."
- Telugu: "సోషల్ మీడియాలో వైరల్ అయితేనే అధికారులు స్పందిస్తున్నారని ప్రజలు అంటున్నారు."

### 9.3 Taxpayer Anger

**Pattern**

Many civic complaints are framed as a taxpayer-rights issue.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "What are we paying taxes for?"
- Tinglish: "Tax kattedi enduku? Basic facilities kuda levu."
- Telugu: "పన్నులు చెల్లిస్తున్నా ప్రాథమిక సౌకర్యాలు అందడం లేదని ప్రజలు ఆగ్రహం వ్యక్తం చేస్తున్నారు."

### 9.4 Demand for Officer and Contractor Accountability

**Pattern**

People do not only want service delivery; they want named accountability for failed works, bad permissions, and repeated negligence.

**Public-language examples**

- English: "Who approved this work and why is there no penalty?"
- Tinglish: "Contractor work poor ga chesadu, official accountability ekkada?"
- Telugu: "నిర్లక్ష్యం చేసిన అధికారులు, కాంట్రాక్టర్లపై చర్యలు తీసుకోవాలి."
